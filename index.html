Artist Statement

My artwork takes a variety aspect of emotions, society and environment issues. Often referencing Asian culture, my work explores the varying relationship between new media art culture and traditional fine art. Having engaged subjects as diverse as the environmental problems, help people Venting their emotions and trying to address some issues in our community, I work reproduces some familiar signs, arranging them into new materials. Thus, I may use a variety of materials and processes in each project. The subject matter of each body of work determines the materials and the forms of the work. I love to use different medium or materials to express the different themes or some specific meanings, While I using dfferent materials, I learned certain new thought and view.
