"Green Space",Dightal Video,2 mins"

This piece is an exploration of the environment and how technology changes the way people view the outdoor space. I was shotting as traveling in a natural area. In the other hands, I create two screens. The main screen is showing the front view, and I used the door as a transmission to guilds the viewer softly enter natural spaces from the indoor environment. The second screen sets down on the floor where the viewer stands; it shows the overlook view from the top and gives an immersive experience to the viewer; especially the video has connects and synchronize the picture with the main screen together.

Currently, I had been suffering in the sorrow of Camp wildfire, and the dark clouds hang over us. San Jose Housing Issue, Documentary Video, 1 hr 30 mins, Guoxun Wu, 2018

This is a group project that record the housing issue in San Jose area today. We interview different people from different class, and created one hour 30 minutes video. And I focus on the issue of student homeless, I interview two people who has experience sleep in car and library, and recorded down the reason and scence in the video.
